## Overview

CommodityNode ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

**Automatically collected:**
- Basic server logs (page URLs, timestamps, referrers, IP addresses)
- Browser type and device type for analytics purposes

**We do not collect:**
- Account registrations (no sign-up required)
- Payment information
- Personal profile data

## Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These tools do not identify individual users.

## Cookies

This site may set minimal functional cookies. We do not use third-party advertising cookies without disclosure.

## Google AdSense

If we display Google AdSense advertisements in the future, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out via [Google's Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Third-Party Services

Content is served via GitHub Pages (Microsoft). Please review [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for details on their data practices.

D3.js and Google Fonts are loaded from external CDNs. These services may log your IP address.

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold by contacting us via the [contact page](/contact/).

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect changes.

## Contact

For privacy inquiries, see our [contact page](/contact/).
