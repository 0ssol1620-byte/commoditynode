<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>CommodityNode ("we", "us", "our") publishes commodity market intelligence at <a href="https://commoditynode.com/">commoditynode.com</a>. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using the site.</p>

<h2>Information We Collect</h2>
<p><strong>Free visitors</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>basic server logs such as page URLs, timestamps, referrers, IP addresses, and user-agent strings</li>
  <li>aggregate analytics signals about device type, browser, and on-site behavior</li>
  <li>email address and message content only when you voluntarily contact us or submit a form</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Registered or paid users</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>account email address and authentication details handled through our account provider</li>
  <li>billing and subscription status handled by our payment processor</li>
  <li>plan metadata needed to enable or restrict product features</li>
</ul>

<h2>What We Do Not Store</h2>
<ul>
  <li>We do not store full payment-card details on CommodityNode servers.</li>
  <li>We do not sell personal data to third parties.</li>
  <li>We do not collect unnecessary personal-profile fields from casual readers.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How We Use Information</h2>
<ul>
  <li>to operate the site and secure the service</li>
  <li>to understand which pages are useful or broken</li>
  <li>to respond to contact requests, corrections, and partnership inquiries</li>
  <li>to administer subscriptions, entitlements, and support requests</li>
  <li>to show advertising and measure aggregate site usage</li>
</ul>

<h2>Cookies and Similar Technologies</h2>
<p>CommodityNode uses limited cookies and browser storage for functional purposes such as analytics, account state, metering, and UI preferences. Some third-party services we rely on may also set their own cookies or local-storage values.</p>

<h2>Analytics</h2>
<p>We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns and improve the site. You can opt out via the <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on</a>.</p>

<h2>Google AdSense</h2>
<p>CommodityNode may display Google AdSense advertisements. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can review controls via <a href="https://www.google.com/settings/ads" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Ad Settings</a>.</p>

<h2>Third-Party Services We Use</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>GitHub Pages</strong> — site hosting</li>
  <li><strong>Google Analytics</strong> — aggregate analytics</li>
  <li><strong>Google AdSense</strong> — advertising</li>
  <li><strong>Clerk</strong> — account authentication and user-session management</li>
  <li><strong>Lemon Squeezy</strong> — billing, checkout, and subscription events</li>
  <li><strong>Formspree</strong> — contact-form handling</li>
  <li><strong>Google Fonts</strong> — typography delivery</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data Retention</h2>
<p>We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, legal, support, or security purposes. Logs and analytics may be retained in aggregate form for site-performance and abuse-prevention analysis.</p>

<h2>Your Choices</h2>
<ul>
  <li>You may contact us to request deletion of personal information we directly control.</li>
  <li>You may use browser settings or extensions to control cookies and ad personalization.</li>
  <li>You may unsubscribe from emails or newsletters using the unsubscribe option included in those communications.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
<p>CommodityNode is intended for adults using market-intelligence content. We do not knowingly target children.</p>

<h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>We may update this policy as the product and vendor stack evolve. The “last updated” date will reflect material revisions.</p>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>For privacy questions, email <a href="mailto:contact@commoditynode.com">contact@commoditynode.com</a> or use the <a href="/contact/">contact page</a>.</p>
