    <h2>How to Reach CommodityNode</h2>
    <p>CommodityNode is run by a small independent publishing team. We welcome thoughtful outreach, especially when it improves the usefulness or accuracy of the site.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Corrections</strong> — factual, numerical, or labeling issues on any page</li>
      <li><strong>News tips</strong> — commodity moves, disruptions, or data points worth covering</li>
      <li><strong>Partnerships</strong> — research collaborations, data partnerships, and publishing opportunities</li>
      <li><strong>Enterprise requests</strong> — team access, exports, onboarding, or workflow questions</li>
      <li><strong>General feedback</strong> — what is working, missing, or confusing</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Primary Email</h3>
    <p>For most inquiries, email <a href="mailto:contact@commoditynode.com">contact@commoditynode.com</a>.</p>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;color:var(--text3);">Typical response window: 2–3 business days. Correction requests usually receive the fastest review.</p>

    <h3>What to Include in a Correction Request</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>the exact page URL</li>
      <li>the value, statement, or label you believe is wrong</li>
      <li>your source or supporting evidence, if available</li>
      <li>why the issue matters for readers</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Enterprise &amp; Team Access</h3>
    <p>For multi-seat access, onboarding, API-style workflows, or data exports, contact <a href="mailto:enterprise@commoditynode.com">enterprise@commoditynode.com</a>.</p>
    <p><a href="/enterprise/">Learn more about Enterprise →</a></p>

    <hr>
    <h2>Editorial &amp; Commercial Transparency</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>CommodityNode publishes independent analysis and does not accept payment for favorable commodity or company coverage.</li>
      <li>Advertising and subscriptions support the site, but sponsored research is not mixed into core market analysis.</li>
      <li>If a feed is rollover-distorted, stale, or proxy-limited, we may suppress the value rather than imply false precision.</li>
    </ul>
