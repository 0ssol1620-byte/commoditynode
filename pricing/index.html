Should you actually pay?

Pay for Pro when commodity moves actually change what you do next.

The strongest buyers are not casual readers. They are people whose watchlists, sector views, or input-cost assumptions move when commodities move.

Pay for Pro if…
  • commodity moves regularly change the stocks or sectors you track
  • you care about event setups like OPEC, WASDE, or Fed-driven metals repricing
  • you need stock-level or scenario-level translation, not just a narrative summary
  • saving one bad decision or one missed setup is worth more than $19
Do not pay yet if…
  • you mainly want generic market headlines or free charting tools
  • you do not act on commodity-linked setups in portfolios, sectors, or budgets
  • you are still evaluating whether the workflow fits how you think
  • you only need passive reading instead of a repeatable decision loop

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the AI forecast?+
Our proprietary AI forecast engine uses state-of-the-art time series models to generate 90-day price projections. Forecasts include 80% confidence intervals so you can assess the uncertainty range. Updated daily with fresh market data.
Is there a billing portal?+
Yes, manage your subscription at any time through your account. View invoices, update payment methods, or change your plan — all self-serve.
What commodities are covered?+
60 commodity hubs including energy (crude oil, natural gas, LNG), precious metals (gold, silver, platinum), industrial metals (copper, aluminum, nickel), agriculture (wheat, corn, soybeans, coffee), and critical materials (lithium, uranium, cobalt).
Is this financial advice?+
No. CommodityNode provides research and analytical tools for informational purposes only. All forecasts and analysis should be treated as one input among many in your investment process. Please read our full disclaimer on the methodology page.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?+
Free gives you access to all 60 commodity hubs, up to 3 Signal Reports per month, plus the public forecast stack and simulator preview context so you can evaluate the workflow before paying. Pro unlocks unlimited Signal Reports, deeper scenario workflow, company sensitivity analysis, and trading notes on each signal.
How is this different from TradingView or free charting tools?+
TradingView and free charting tools are excellent for seeing price. CommodityNode is for the next step: translating a commodity move into exposed sectors, names, and event scenarios before the market fully reprices.
How is this different from reading commodity news?+
News tells you what happened. CommodityNode is designed to help you decide what the move changes: which sectors absorb it, which names likely benefit or get hurt, and whether the setup deserves deeper scenario work.
When is Pro most useful?+
Pro is most useful when the move is important enough that you care about stock-level exposure, forecast disagreement, and scenario confidence — not just a broad narrative or chart.
What happens after the 7-day trial?+
Your account automatically converts to the Free tier. No charge. You keep access to all commodity hubs, public reports, the public forecast stack, and simulator preview context while you decide whether Pro belongs in your workflow.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click. No questions, no exit interview.
Do you offer refunds?+
Yes, we offer a full refund within the first 14 days if Pro isn't right for you.
Is there an API?+
API access is available on the Enterprise plan. Contact us for documentation and rate limits.
How does Enterprise work?+
Contact us for team seats, API access, and custom data exports. We'll respond within 24 hours. Enterprise includes everything in Pro plus dedicated support and custom integrations.
Is this suitable for individual investors, not just professionals?+
Yes. Most Pro members are individual investors and self-directed traders who want the same commodity intelligence that professional analysts use — without institutional pricing. If you manage a personal portfolio with commodity-sensitive stocks (energy, mining, agriculture, airlines), Pro pays for itself quickly.

CommodityNode is priced to be accessible. We'd rather have 1,000 analysts using us daily at $19/month than 10 institutions paying $1,000/month who barely use the product. The best research tool is one you actually open before making a decision.
