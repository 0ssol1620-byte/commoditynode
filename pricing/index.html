Frequently asked questions

Our proprietary AI forecast engine uses state-of-the-art time series models to generate 90-day price projections. Forecasts include 80% confidence intervals so you can assess the uncertainty range. Updated daily with fresh market data.
How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

Yes, manage your subscription at any time through your account. View invoices, update payment methods, or change your plan — all self-serve.
What commodities are covered?
60 commodity hubs including energy (crude oil, natural gas, LNG), precious metals (gold, silver, platinum), industrial metals (copper, aluminum, nickel), agriculture (wheat, corn, soybeans, coffee), and critical materials (lithium, uranium, cobalt).
Is this financial advice?
No. CommodityNode provides research and analytical tools for informational purposes only. All forecasts and analysis should be treated as one input among many in your investment process. Please read our full disclaimer on the methodology page.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
Free gives you access to all 60 commodity hubs, up to 3 Signal Reports per month, and the basic Impact Map. Pro unlocks unlimited Signal Reports, the AI 90-day price forecast, full Scenario Simulator with company sensitivity analysis, and trading notes on each signal.
What happens after the 7-day trial?
Your account automatically converts to the Free tier. No charge. You keep access to all commodity hubs, public reports, and the basic Impact Map.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click. No questions, no exit interview.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a full refund within the first 14 days if Pro isn't right for you.
Is there an API?
API access is available on the Enterprise plan. Contact us for documentation and rate limits.
How does Enterprise work?
Contact us for team seats, API access, and custom data exports. We'll respond within 24 hours. Enterprise includes everything in Pro plus dedicated support and custom integrations.
Is this suitable for individual investors, not just professionals?
Yes. Most Pro members are individual investors and self-directed traders who want the same commodity intelligence that professional analysts use — without institutional pricing. If you manage a personal portfolio with commodity-sensitive stocks (energy, mining, agriculture, airlines), Pro pays for itself quickly.
