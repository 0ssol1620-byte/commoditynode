Why access passes instead of auto-renewing subscriptions?

Initial paid access is structured as time-limited research access to reduce payment-review and customer-dispute risk while the product validates demand.

Can I pay by card or PayPal?

PayPal and global card integrations are being prepared with server-side payment verification. Until then, access requests are handled manually or by enterprise invoice.

Is this for traders?

The product can be used by market watchers, but it is positioned as commodity market intelligence and scenario analytics, not trade recommendations.
