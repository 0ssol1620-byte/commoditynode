Price Sensitivity

AngloGold Ashanti's earnings are directly leveraged to gold prices, with approximately 0.85 correlation to spot gold on a rolling 12-month basis. A $100/oz move in gold translates to roughly $500-700 million in annual revenue impact. The company's all-in sustaining costs of approximately $1,200-1,400/oz determine the operating leverage -- every dollar above AISC flows nearly directly to cash flow.
