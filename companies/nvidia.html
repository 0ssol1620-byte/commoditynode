Company Overview

The world's most valuable semiconductor company, dominating AI training and inference chip markets with its GPU architecture, driving unprecedented demand for data center power and cooling.

Commodity Exposures

Copper Gold Natural Gas Silver

Price Sensitivity

NVIDIA's direct commodity exposure comes through TSMC's manufacturing (silicon, copper, gold for packaging) and its own system-level products (DGX servers consuming copper, aluminum, and steel). However, NVIDIA's indirect commodity impact is far larger: the AI data centers powered by NVIDIA GPUs are driving unprecedented electricity demand, with a single AI training cluster consuming as much power as a small city. This is creating massive demand for natural gas, uranium, copper (for power delivery), and cooling infrastructure.
