Company Overview

America's largest electric utility by market capitalization and the world's largest generator of wind and solar energy through its NextEra Energy Resources subsidiary.

Commodity Exposures

Natural Gas Copper Silver Steel

Price Sensitivity

NextEra Energy operates dual commodity exposures. Florida Power & Light, the regulated utility, passes through natural gas fuel costs to ratepayers but faces regulatory lag. NextEra Energy Resources, the renewables subsidiary, is a massive consumer of construction commodities (copper, steel, silver for solar, rare earths for wind turbines). The renewables buildout creates upfront commodity demand, but once operational, renewable assets have zero fuel cost exposure -- a powerful long-term hedge against fossil fuel commodity volatility.
