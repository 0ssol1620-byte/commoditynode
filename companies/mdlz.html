Company Overview

Mondelez International operates in premium snacking with iconic brands: Oreo, Cadbury, Toblerone, Milka, Chips Ahoy, Ritz, belVita, and Triscuit. Cocoa represents approximately 40% of net revenues and is the most critical commodity input. Mondelez purchases roughly 15% of global cocoa beans annually, making it one of the world's largest cocoa buyers. Sugar, wheat, and palm oil are secondary inputs. The company's brand portfolio commands pricing power allowing partial recovery of commodity cost increases, though pricing actions typically lag by 2-4 quarters.

Price Sensitivity

The 2023-2024 cocoa price surge to $10,000+/tonne (from ~$2,500 historical average) represented an unprecedented margin shock. Each 10% increase in cocoa prices creates approximately $300M in annual gross profit headwind. Sugar and wheat are secondary inputs representing smaller total exposure. MDLZ's multinational operations create currency exposure across 80+ countries, with euro, British pound, and emerging market currencies being most significant. The company's pricing power and brand strength allow eventual margin recovery but with meaningful lag.
