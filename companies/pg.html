Company Overview

The world's largest consumer goods company, producing household and personal care brands including Tide, Pampers, Gillette, and Olay across 180+ countries.

Commodity Exposures

Crude Oil Natural Gas Palm Oil Cotton

Price Sensitivity

P&G's commodity exposure spans petroleum-derived chemicals (surfactants, plastics for packaging), natural gas (energy and chemical feedstock), palm and coconut oil (soap and detergent ingredients), cotton and cellulose (paper products), and packaging materials (aluminum, cardboard). Commodity costs represent approximately 50% of cost of goods sold. P&G's brand strength allows significant pricing power to pass through commodity inflation, but with a typical 6-12 month lag.
