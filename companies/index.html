World's largest gold miner — gold price is the dominant earnings variable.

Diversified mining giant — iron ore, copper, coal, and nickel across global operations.

Global mining leader — iron ore dominant with aluminum and copper growth segments.

Pure-play aluminum — bauxite mining through smelting and rolling operations.

Nucor Corporation

Largest U.S. steelmaker — electric arc furnace model with scrap and iron ore inputs.

Farm equipment leader — sales correlate with grain prices and farm income cycles.
