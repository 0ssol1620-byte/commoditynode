BYD Company (BYD)

{{ page.description }}

Company Overview

Chinese EV and battery manufacturer that has become the world's largest EV seller, vertically integrated from battery cell production through complete vehicle assembly.

Commodity Exposures

Lithium Copper Iron Nickel

Price Sensitivity

BYD's vertical integration from battery cells (Blade Battery using LFP chemistry) through complete vehicles gives it unique commodity exposure. BYD's LFP batteries use iron and phosphate instead of nickel and cobalt, providing a cost advantage and different commodity exposure than competitors using NMC chemistry. The company's massive scale (3+ million vehicles annually) makes it one of the world's largest consumers of lithium and copper.
