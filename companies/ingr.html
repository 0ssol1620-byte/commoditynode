Company Overview

Ingredion is the world's leading specialty ingredient solutions company, converting corn, tapioca, wheat, and other grains into starches, sweeteners, proteins, and specialty ingredients for food, beverage, paper, and industrial sectors. Operating 56 facilities across 30+ countries, Ingredion serves 20,000+ customers globally. North America contributes ~60% of revenue with significant South American and Asia-Pacific operations. The specialty ingredients segment — clean-label, plant-based proteins, functional starches — is growing faster than commodity sweeteners and provides better margins.

Price Sensitivity

Corn is the primary input — Ingredion wet-mills approximately 10% of US industrial corn. A $1/bushel change in corn prices impacts EBITDA by approximately $100M at North American volumes. The specialty ingredients segment (~30% of volumes) commands better margins and is less sensitive to corn price pass-through. Geographic diversification means local corn prices in South America and Asia are also significant cost drivers independent of CBOT corn. The company hedges corn purchases 3-6 months forward on average.
