Company Overview

Dutch company and the world's sole manufacturer of extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography systems essential for producing the most advanced semiconductor chips.

Commodity Exposures

Copper Steel Natural Gas

Price Sensitivity

ASML's EUV lithography machines are among the most complex devices ever manufactured, each containing over 100,000 parts and costing $200+ million. The machines themselves consume significant specialty metals and optics materials. More importantly, ASML enables the entire advanced semiconductor manufacturing chain -- without EUV systems, the chips powering AI data centers couldn't be made. ASML's order backlog is a leading indicator of future semiconductor capacity and the associated commodity demand for fab construction and operation.
