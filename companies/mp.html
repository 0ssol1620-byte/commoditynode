Company Overview

MP Materials operates Mountain Pass, California — the richest rare earth deposit in the Western Hemisphere and the only integrated rare earth mine and processor outside China with scale. Mountain Pass produces rare earth concentrate processed into NdPr (neodymium-praseodymium) oxide used in permanent magnets for EV motors, wind turbines, and defense electronics. The Fort Worth, Texas magnet manufacturing facility (in development) will complete the full supply chain from mine to finished magnet. US government strategic partnerships provide demand visibility and policy support.

Price Sensitivity

NdPr oxide price is the critical earnings driver — each $10/kg change impacts annual EBITDA by approximately $50M. MP is uniquely positioned as the only significant non-Chinese rare earth supplier with scale, though China's pricing power through dominant production creates competitive risk. The magnet manufacturing strategy aims to capture higher downstream margins versus commodity concentrate sales. US-China trade tensions and critical minerals legislation are structural tailwinds for Mountain Pass's strategic value.
