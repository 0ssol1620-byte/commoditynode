Company Overview

The world's most valuable company, designing and manufacturing consumer electronics including iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Apple Watch, with a massive services ecosystem.

Commodity Exposures

Copper Gold Aluminum Lithium

Price Sensitivity

Apple consumes significant quantities of aluminum (MacBook and iPad enclosures), copper (circuit boards and wiring), gold (connector plating), lithium (batteries), and rare earth elements (speakers, haptic engines). Apple's scale means it purchases approximately 1% of global aluminum foil production annually. The company's commitment to recycled materials is driving innovation in commodity recovery but hasn't reduced absolute commodity dependence given growing unit volumes.
