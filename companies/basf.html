Company Overview

German chemical giant and the world's largest chemical company by revenue, operating across chemicals, materials, industrial solutions, surface technologies, nutrition, and agricultural solutions.

Commodity Exposures

Natural Gas Crude Oil Platinum

Price Sensitivity

BASF's massive Ludwigshafen Verbund site in Germany is one of the world's largest chemical complexes and is highly dependent on natural gas both as energy and feedstock. The 2022 European gas crisis directly threatened BASF's operations, forcing production curtailments. BASF's Catalysts division is the world's largest producer of automotive catalytic converters, creating significant exposure to platinum, palladium, and rhodium prices.
