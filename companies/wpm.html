Wheaton Precious Metals (WPM)

{{ page.description }}

Company Overview

The world's largest precious metals streaming company, providing upfront capital to miners in exchange for the right to purchase a percentage of production at predetermined prices.

Commodity Exposures

Gold Silver Palladium

Price Sensitivity

Wheaton's streaming model provides leveraged exposure to gold and silver prices with lower operating risk than traditional miners. With cash costs of approximately $5/oz for silver and $250/oz for gold, Wheaton's margins expand dramatically as precious metal prices rise. The company has streaming agreements with 23 operating mines, providing diversified production without direct mine operating risk.
