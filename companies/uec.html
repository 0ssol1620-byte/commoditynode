Company Overview

Uranium Energy Corp is the largest US-based uranium producer by mineral resource, operating ISR (in-situ recovery) uranium mines in Texas (Palangana, Burke Hollow, Goliad) and Wyoming (Reno Creek). ISR extraction pumps oxygenated water underground to dissolve uranium — the lowest-cost production method with minimal surface disturbance. UEC's extensive permitted portfolio positions it to rapidly scale production when prices justify expansion. Physical uranium inventory provides direct price leverage without production risk. The US government uranium reserve program provides a demand floor.

Price Sensitivity

UEC has the highest uranium price leverage among major producers with no systematic hedging, providing maximum upside exposure. Near 1:1 correlation between uranium spot price and UEC share performance is typical. Reno Creek in Wyoming is the largest permitted uranium project in the US, capable of 3.6Mlbs/year production. UEC benefits from US government efforts to rebuild domestic uranium supply chains under defense and energy security policy frameworks.
