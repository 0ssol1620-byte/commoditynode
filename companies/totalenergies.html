Company Overview

French integrated energy major with a diversified portfolio spanning oil, natural gas, LNG, and one of the largest renewable energy pipelines among supermajors.

Commodity Exposures

Crude Oil Natural Gas

Price Sensitivity

TotalEnergies has the most diversified energy mix among European supermajors, with significant positions in solar, wind, and battery storage alongside traditional oil and gas. However, upstream oil and gas production still drives 70%+ of earnings. The company's large LNG portfolio provides exposure to both European TTF and Asian gas prices.
