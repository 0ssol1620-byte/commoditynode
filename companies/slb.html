Price Sensitivity

SLB's revenue is a lagged function of oil and gas prices -- when commodity prices rise, E&P companies increase drilling activity 3-6 months later, driving oilfield services demand. SLB's digital and technology-focused business model provides higher margins than traditional services, but the fundamental driver remains global drilling activity, which is ultimately determined by commodity prices.
