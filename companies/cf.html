Company Overview

The world's largest producer of nitrogen fertilizer products, with nine manufacturing complexes in the US, UK, and Canada. Products include ammonia, granular urea, UAN, and ammonium nitrate.

Commodity Exposures

Natural Gas Corn Wheat

Price Sensitivity

CF Industries' profitability is fundamentally determined by the spread between natural gas input costs and nitrogen fertilizer selling prices. As a US-based producer with access to low-cost Henry Hub natural gas, CF has a structural cost advantage over European competitors facing higher TTF gas prices. Grain prices drive fertilizer demand -- when corn and wheat prices are high, farmers increase nitrogen application rates.
