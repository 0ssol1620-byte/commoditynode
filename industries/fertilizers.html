Industry Overview

Fertilizer manufacturing is perhaps the most directly commodity-linked industry outside of mining and energy extraction. Natural gas is both the feedstock and energy source for ammonia production (the basis of all nitrogen fertilizers), representing 70-80% of ammonia production costs. Phosphate and potash mining operations feed the other two legs of the NPK fertilizer trinity. Fertilizer demand is driven by grain prices: when corn and wheat prices are high, farmers increase fertilizer application to maximize yields. This creates a powerful positive feedback loop between agricultural commodity prices and fertilizer demand that amplifies commodity cycles.
