# AdSense Re-Review Checklist

## Goal
Raise CommodityNode's perceived content quality, trust, and originality before requesting another AdSense review.

## Completed in this remediation round
- Strengthened About, Contact, and Methodology transparency surfaces
- Added explicit correction/review/data-quality policy language
- Reduced sitemap noise from noindex tag pages
- Hardened live price handling to avoid false precision on unstable feeds
- Selectively removed analysis-only long-tail commodity pages from sitemap/index priority

## Before requesting review

### 1. Trust & policy surfaces
- [x] About page explains who runs the site and how analysis is produced
- [x] Methodology page explains data sources and limitations
- [x] Contact page provides real contact path and correction workflow
- [x] Terms / Privacy pages are publicly accessible
- [x] Editorial and commercial separation is stated clearly

### 2. Content quality
- [ ] Review top 20 most important landing pages manually for repetitive template feel
- [ ] Expand at least 5 cornerstone pages with unique insight blocks, citations, and examples
- [ ] Ensure key pages answer a real user question, not just describe a commodity generically
- [ ] Reduce thin or low-differentiation pages from active index priority

### 3. Monetization readiness
- [ ] Avoid requesting review while large numbers of low-value pages are being generated automatically
- [ ] Make sure core pages look finished without requiring login
- [ ] Keep ad placements conservative until policy approval risk is lower

### 4. Technical quality
- [x] Noindex pages should also be excluded from sitemap where applicable
- [ ] Re-submit sitemap in Google Search Console after major index-policy changes
- [ ] Inspect a sample of pages in URL Inspection for canonical/index signals
- [ ] Verify no broken internal links on key landing pages

### 5. Manual final check before resubmission
- [ ] Home
- [ ] About
- [ ] Methodology
- [ ] Contact
- [ ] Pricing
- [ ] Reports index
- [ ] Gold hub
- [ ] Crude oil hub
- [ ] Copper hub
- [ ] Natural gas hub
- [ ] 3 recent reports

## Recommendation
Do not request AdSense re-review immediately after a technical cleanup alone. First strengthen a small set of flagship pages so Google sees clearer evidence of unique, user-first value.
