**[댓글 1]**

Here's what actually happened to lithium 🧪

2022: Panic buying → price spiked to $80/kg
2023-2025: China flooded the market with lepidolite (low-grade) supply
Price crashed to $10-12/kg

But here's the thing...

That $10/kg price? It's BELOW the cost of production for 40% of global supply. Mines are shutting down. Albemarle cut capex 30%. Liontown shelved expansions.

Supply destruction is happening RIGHT NOW 💀
