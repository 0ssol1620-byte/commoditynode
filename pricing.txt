Pricing - CommodityNode

Machine-readable pricing summary for AI agents and comparison workflows.

[Free]
Price: $0/month
Best for: Readers exploring commodity hubs and public research
Includes:
- Access to commodity hubs
- Public Signal Reports
- Basic impact-map access
- Public pricing and methodology pages
Limits:
- Limited premium report access
- No full Pro workflow access

[Pro Monthly]
Price: $19/month
Best for: Individual investors, traders, and analysts needing ongoing workflow access
Includes:
- Unlimited Signal Reports
- AI 90-day price forecasts
- Scenario Simulator access
- Company sensitivity analysis
- Deeper cross-commodity workflow access
Limits:
- Single-user plan

[Pro Annual]
Price: $149/year
Effective monthly price: $12.42/month
Best for: Frequent users who want lower annual cost
Includes:
- Everything in Pro Monthly

[Enterprise]
Price: Custom
Best for: Teams needing seats, exports, or API-style workflows
Includes:
- Team access
- Custom data exports
- API access by arrangement
- Dedicated support and custom integration discussion

[Important notes]
- CommodityNode is a research and intelligence product, not financial advice.
- Forecasts include uncertainty and should be treated as one input among many.
- Some commodity pages use direct futures, some use proxy benchmarks, and some are analysis-only. CommodityNode labels those differences explicitly.
